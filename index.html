About Street Lights

January 29, 2018 0

Street lights is a group of Ugandan software developers that work together to develop software for sectors that have been ignored.

The organization started as a pet project by Software Engineers of the year 2012-2016 from Makerere University as our give back to the community. Our first project is more local to Uganda as an aim to provide a platform where people can learn to speak local languages spoken in Uganda. The first use-case being Luganda which is Uganda's most widely spoken local language.
